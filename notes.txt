## Configuration Tips
- Switch models in .env: change OLLAMA_MODEL to phi3, mistral, etc.
- Tune CHUNK_SIZE (default 500) for your log format
- Adjust CHUNK_OVERLAP for better context continuity

## Notes
- FAISS is used as the vector store for fast local similarity search
- Streaming is implemented via Server-Sent Events (SSE) in Flask
- All processing happens locally — no data leaves the machine
